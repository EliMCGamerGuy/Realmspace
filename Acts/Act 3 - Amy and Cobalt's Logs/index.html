    Amethyst's Experiment Log 1¶

    (Beep!)
    (MON - 10/4/5AU - 10PM)
    (FffssSHSHHHHhhh...)
    (The scene suddenly gets bathed in light as Amethyst lights a candle, the flame making her pure black hands glow a very dark purple. Her pure black hair and dark purple streak are tied back in a loose ponytail. She holds up the match and waves it out. The camera is on Amy's desk, and in the background is a bunk bed, the top bunk messier than the lower, and a window in the back wall with darkness outside. Amy picks up her pen and slides a note page to the center of the cleared portion of her desk.)
    "Saul couldn't hold the rift open for very long, nor could he open it very wide. Opening rifts does require a certain amount of strength."
    (She writes something down on a sheet of note paper.)
    "But I have my doubts that Saul would be unable to pull open a rift now. He's is the resistance's head mechanic, handyman, and metalworker. He used to be our best realmspace navigator before rifts became ineffective. He didn't even use a map, he just used the realms' stars to guide him.

    Hmm...I guess it's not outside the realm of possibility, though."

    (A slim humanlike kni girl with shoulder-length black hair, a black t-shirt, and dark gray shorts drops down silently from the top bunk, and takes a detour specifically to not come near Amy while passing.)
    (The room's door opens then closes out of shot.)
    "Rooming with Cobalt as of a few days ago. Housing officer placed her here because we have history together. She's...not much for conversation anymore, she's a salvage runner for the resistance now."
    (Amy sets a little metal stand over top the candle, on top of which she puts a small metal pot. She pours water from a canteen into the small pot, and puts a tea bag into it.)
    "I'm going to see if I can design some sort of machine to physically pull the rift open. It's worth a shot."
    (Amy reaches over to the camera.)
    (Beep!)
