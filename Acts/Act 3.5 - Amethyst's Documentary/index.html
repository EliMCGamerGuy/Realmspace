    (THU - 12/9/5AU - 1PM)

    ( Amethyst Silver )
    "The date is Firmont 17th, 1533.

    A replica kni camp in the eastern Crystallyne area is empty. Not due to lack of food or water, or a camp relocation, no. They went north and met another replica kni camp in the northeastern Crystallyne area. Meeting them there as well were the warriors and able-bodied of all of the nearby camps. They've taken all their possessions with them. They don't intend to return.

    The Crystallyne guard are not unaware of this assembly. They double their stationed guard. Seeing smoke billowing from the northern forest, they prepare for the worst. Unfortunately, they come to would grossly underestimate their foe.

    This clash, come to be known as the Replica Kni Uprising of 1534AM, or just "the uprising" for brevity, will change several lives permanently, over not even a year. It would spell a new era for kni and humanity alike in realm 04."

    (THU - 12/10/5AU - 2PM)

    ( Amethyst Silver )
    "Alright. Can you tell us about yourself, Lieutenant Mason?"

    ( Lt. Andrew Mason )
    "Sure. My name is Lt. Andrew Mason. I am a humanlike kni in the Crystallyne guard. Or...what's left of it. Now I run recon for the resistance, mostly."

    ( Amy )
    "Thanks. Now, if you had to sum up the uprising in one sentence, what would it be?"

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "Hmmh. Brutal, bloody, and unnecessary."

    ( Amy )
    "Do you remember where you were stationed on Sirmont 17th, 1533?"

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "Yep. Station 58, near the northern city limit. Been there for about 3 years. After that, they changed my station to station 27, near the northeastern city limit, where the perimeter had been set up. On the 18th the replica kni made contact with the first line initial guards, and we had front row seats. Replica kni streaming down the northeastern hill as foxes, wolves, tigers, and kni. They were overwhelmed almost immediately, and we had to step in to hold the perimeter."

    ( Amy )
    "What do you think the guard underestimated the most, the amount of replica kni or their unity?"

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "Probably both, but mostly their unity. Until then, we had never seen the replica kni come together at such a scale. Even today, we have wolf packs patrolling the streets, never alone. But there were also much more replica kni then than we expected. The working theory is that there were more replica kni camps over the northeastern hill, past the existing camp."

    ( Amy )
    "You said that the guard was overwhelmed. Can you say how you felt, in that moment?"

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "None of us had known that we were overwhelmed until it was too late. We all had to hold the line, unaware that the line was slowly creeping backwards. When we realized we were overwhelmed, we- at least, I- felt hopeless as the perimeter faded."

    ( Amy )
    "Was there ever a moment that you thought to yourself that the guard could still win?"

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "Yeah. We're trained for battle. But...not quite battle against replica kni as animals. The morale hit when we knew that the initial guards were overwhelmed was huge, but it also drove us to fight harder. For a few seconds, we all felt unstoppable."

    ( Amy )
    "How did your superiors react when the perimeter was breached?"

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "Oh. Panic, mostly. They rushed out an evac notice and it stirred up chaos.

    ...I like to think it might've helped some people, though."

    ( Amy )
    "And...And it's ok if you don't want to answer this one...Is there a moment that keeps you up at night?"

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "Yes, there is. Good buddy of mine...Corporal Thomas Packer. Tom. He was in the line with me not too far off. This gets kind of gross, are you sure?"

    ( Amy )
    "I can edit it."

    ( Lt. Mason )
    "Ok.

    He had taken a replica kni's arm off with his sword. This is typically a pretty important pick, they don't usually know heal focus. So the replica kni doubled over, but then collected some of his essence in his hand, throwing it at Tom, catching him off guard.

    The replica kni took the moment of vulnerability and speared Tom in the torso with his off-hand. I fought through as fast as I could and carried Tom back to the aid station, but...it was full.

    They said that the spear had gone through his core and he'd lost too much essence to be stable enough to wait. There was nothing they could do.
