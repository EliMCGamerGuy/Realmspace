    Realmspace¶

    Intro¶

    What if there was more worlds out there, past a special plane of existence that we humans alone cannot cross?

    ...

    What if there was a hostile takeover in one of those worlds?
    How would that affect the people in that world?

    What is Realmspace?¶

    Realmspace, an ongoing passion project by EliMCGamerGuy, is a character-driven story about the people after an uprising, told through logs, journals, recordings, and otherwise written or recorded word or action.

    It is written by me alone, with the characters and lore living almost full lives in my mind. For more information, I would recommend reading the Realmspace AI Statement.

    ! Realmspace is a work of fiction. !¶

    References, names, or similarities to real people or pre-existing characters, unless directly intended, are coincidental.

    Content warnings:¶

    I've never actually thought about the requirement of content warnings, but Realmspace doesn't play coy with a number of topics.
    Here are some topics that its brushed with, including unreleased in-development acts.

    • Blood, essence, and possible described gore
    • Described or implied violence
    • Emotional distress
    • Depression
    • Suicide contemplation
    • PTSD or trauma
    • Loss or separation from family members
    • Mass emotional repercussions of a hostile takeover
    • Alcohol use
    • Mention of addiction
    • Brief inebriation/drunkenness
    • Intimidation
    • Genocide
    • Character death
    • Description of non-biological focus-based kni reproduction(alloying)
    • Partial nudity in requirement for medical aid
    • Possible sexual innuendo(might be in there somewhere...)
    • Caretaking while physically vulnerable
    • Descriptions of medical procedures, injuries
    • Censored swearing
    • Uncensored conlang(Knilang) swearing
    • Found family dynamic
    • Themes of hope, sacrifice, and patience
    • Possible Christian theming

    This list exists to inform potential readers, not to give away story beats.
    The topics that Realmspace brushes with, I try to stay true to examples of and try to portray well.
