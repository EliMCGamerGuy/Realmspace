    Act 1 - Copper Steel's Journal¶

    Annotated by Copper Steel in 5AU

    Entry 1 (5/10/1533) (1BU)¶

    Mom gave me this journal when I turned 17, saying that it's a good thing to write things down. Well, when have I ever denied her suggestion?...don't answer that. I'm not only getting to it a year later.

    (Sapphire Brass)
    Mom. Heh. She'd claim me if she heard me call her by name. Mom's a monklike kni, like me, though I guess that's pretty obvious. She taught me how to make those illusion light orbs. They're really useful, I use them a lot nowadays.

    Entry 2 (5/17/1533) (1BU)¶

    This week, we were tasked with counting how many kni to humans are in the school. We found that it's roughly a 66 - 33 split, but most people don't usually opt take focus classes, so it's more like a 85 to 15% split.

    Our focus histories teacher said that those numbers should be higher, just in case people need to fend off weaponized focus.

    Entry 3 (5/24/1533) (1BU)¶

    John and Jessie ran a rock-shears-paper tournament this week. They had all players go best of three with his or her set opponent. A kni called Platinum won, I only made it to the semifinals.

    (Johnathan Hayes)
    John's a funny one. Brown hair. From a distance, you'd swear he was a humanlike, but he's only human. Opted to take the more focus-oriented classes, though. Twins with Jessie.

    (Jessica Hayes)
    Also known as Jessie. Or Jess. Or "You" by John. Jessie's a human, but by the way she holds a sword, you'd swear she focused it with every ounce of her energy. You wouldn't think so at first by looking at her, but she's rather strong. Twins with John.

    Entry 4 (5/31/1533) (1BU)¶

    It's sparring tournament week! We were all given these wooden swords and shields, and had to back the other out of a circle or knock the sword out of their hand. Dad attended when I was up. I feel like him being there drove me further, but a kid named Kai knocked me out anyway. Oh well. Dad said "You can't win them all," when we were leaving.

    (Steel Brass)
    Dad. Officer in Crystallyne's guard. Monklike kni, like mom and I. Mom sewed on some armored padding to the inside of his robe. I know his dark gray mustache anywhere.

    Entry 5 (6/7/1533) (1BU)¶

    This week, we learned the basics of heal. I'm not very good at it so far, but I'm able to heal paper cuts pretty well. Kni can't get infections but humans can, so to safely heal them you must first wash the wound then use heal.

    I feel sorry for the people who help with this class...

    Entry 6 (6/14/1533) (1BU)¶

    When I got back home on the 14th, dad seemed to be worried while reading the paper. He had just gotten back from work, his armored robe hung up. I didn't ask him what was wrong as I could tell from how he was sitting that he didn't want to be disturbed. Mom had noticed it too, I asked her later on. I also asked her if she knew what was worrying him, but she said didn't know.

    Entry 7 (6/21/1533) (1BU)¶

    John and Jessie had a falling out of sorts. This week is combat focus class, so they decided to spar on it...Jessie won.

    Entry 8 (6/28/1533) (1BU)¶

    Cobalt, Amethyst, and I learned vine this month. I still don't have that good of a grasp on it. Cobalt actually learned how to make ropes with the vines! I don't know if they were strong at all, she didn't let me test it. We also learned that ancient humanlike kni used to use vine and grow to create houses and entire towns made out of pure nature. I'm not usually one for history, but that's pretty cool. Unfortunately, such structures were not well suited for natural disasters.

    (Cobalt Wilton)
    I don't know too much about Cobalt. She's a slim humanlike kni with black hair. Black's an unusual hair color for humanlike, but it's not my business. She's rather quiet. She's friends with Amethyst, though. Sister of Ferrite.

    (Amethyst Silver)
    Also known as Amy, Amethyst is a monklike kni. Amethyst was almost always near the top of our class.

    Entry 9 (7/5/1533) (1BU)¶

    We're on summer break now :)
    I went to the park with Zinc. He brought a board game he imported. It was...I won't sugarcoat it, I hate realm 10's Monopoly.

    (Zinc Lithium)
    I've known Zinc for as long as I can remember. Zinc's a monklike kni. We've actually been asked a time or two if we're related. Nope, just good friends.

    Entry 10 (7/12/1533) (1BU)¶

    Many times this month I've heard dad through the walls. Mom's been trying to figure out why he's worried, no matter the cost. He's worried about the "guard situation," and says he can't go in depth about it. Mom and I hope that he gets it figured out soon.

    Entry 11 (7/19/1533) (1BU)¶

    Zinc's still worse at battleship than me! Granted, I didn't do too well either, haha!

    Entry 12 (7/26/1533) (1BU)¶

    Our group had a picnic this afternoon. Cobalt's brother's a little strange...He didn't want to sit down, opting instead to lean against the tree, even after I cleared him a space on the blanket.

    Cobalt denied to elaborate when I asked, and that's fine. It's probably personal.

    Entry 13 (8/2/1533) (1BU)¶

    Zinc's slightly younger cousin invited us to the nearby lake along with a friend of hers named Luke. I'm going to be honest, I'm getting a little lost thinking about how Shelia's related to Zinc, but I guess that's irrelevant. Don't know who Luke is, but I guess I will soon.

    (Shelia Rogers)
    Shelia is a humanlike kni who's also Zinc's cousin. Don't think about that relation too hard. She's also about a year older than me.

    (Luke Daniels)
    Brown hair, suggestion of facial hair. Luke's a humanlike kni. He's not related to us.

    Entry 14 (8/9/1533) (1BU)¶

    Turns out Shelia & Luke are a bit more than friends. Didn't know that, do now. Felt a little awkward. Luke's cool. I didn't talk with him much, though. Not my place to make a judgement anyway.

    Entry 15 (8/16/1533) (1BU)¶

    Dad sat us down last night, and told us the situation. He said that he really shouldn't, but mom's persistence has really been getting to him. Apparently, unrest has been building in replica kni tribes. The Crystallyne guard fear they may stage an attack. Dad hopes not. He says their combined power might be too much for the city.

    Entry 16 (8/23/1533) (1BU)¶

    don't play uno with zinc

    Entry 17 (8/30/1533) (1BU)¶

    Back to school next week. I was out with John and Jessie. While I was talking, I misspoke, and almost said that I have feelings for Amethyst(which I guess is true). After John and Jessie pulled it out of me, I confessed to them that I have feelings for her, but don't know how to tell her. They decided that they'll figure something out next week. I fear what they have in mind.

    Entry 18 (9/6/1533) (1BU)¶

    John and Jessie plotted out an entire plan to make the situation perfect for me to tell Amy that I have feelings for her. John stood by the sidelines and Jessie stood next to me as I used my locker. Amy came by and Jessie nudged me with her elbow, hard. It almost knocked me over, but thankfully it didn't. I then looked over to Amy as she was using her locker and told her that I had feelings for her. I was nervous and stumbled over my words, but after a few awkward seconds, she accepted. I could see John in the distance pump his fist in the air. Amy insisted that I start calling her Amy instead of Amethyst, though.

    ...John was literally moral support.

    And he pulled it off, too.

    Entry 19 (9/13/1533) (1BU)¶

    Dad didn't come back from work on the 10th. Mom and I are eagerly awaiting his return.

    Entry 20 (9/20/1533) (1BU)¶

    We're starting to worry about dad. He hasn't been back in a week. It's really eerie.

    Sparring tournament this week. It didn't feel right without dad there. I didn't do great.

    Entry 21 (9/27/1533) (1BU)¶

    Mom keeps dishing out dinner for dad, but he still hasn't returned. She just keeps wrapping the bowls. Then they go bad in a few days.

    Entry 22 (10/4/1533) (1BU)¶

    I turned 18 three days ago. Mom told me about alloying today. She said that dad would be able to phrase it better, but she said that if both kni parents focus their energy into one spot, typically while kneeling and holding hands, there's a chance that an alloy will be created. Not a metallic alloy, but another kni. She said that sometimes, to up the chances, some kni will dust their hands with light-colored ash. She also said that since the two genders of kni have different focus signatures, it takes two to alloy. Two male kni cannot alloy, neither can two female. She added that human reproduction is similar, but very different at the same time. She didn't go into depth about that, guess that's up to my own research.

    Sidenote: Jessie and John got rather embarrassed when I asked about the human analog of alloying. John just set his head on the table. Jessie told me, red-faced but smiling, that the cafeteria is not a place for that conversation. That's not a publicly acceptable topic, duly noted. Could've told me that too, MOM.

    Entry 23 (10/11/1533) (1BU)¶

    It's been a month since dad didn't return. We're...starting to really worry. I'm trying to push speculating what happened out of my mind. Nobody has touched his chair in the living room. It's starting to get dusty.

    Entry 24 (10/18/1533) (1BU)¶

    Remember: Amy's birthday is on the 20th of this month.

    Entry 25 (10/25/1533) (1BU)¶

    Didn't forget :)

    Entry 26 (11/1/1533) (1BU)¶

    This week was spear aim practice. You're supposed to counteract spear drop. A spear going about the speed of an arrow may not have much drop in close to mid range, but long range will ruin your aim if you're not adjusting for it. I'm no longer missing the targets entirely at mid range anymore, but I can't quite get long range yet. They always end up sticking into the ground.

    Entry 27 (11/1/1533) (1BU)¶

    For the life class this week, we took a tour of a hospital. I'm going to be honest, I didn't like the "clean" smell. We're going to be quizzed next life week about the uses of the life class in a hospital.

    Nobody with the names Steel or Brass. I checked.

    Entry 28 (11/8/1533) (1BU)¶

    Slept through another focus histories...That's not going to look good.

    Entry 29 (11/15/1533) (1BU)¶

    This week was shield focus practice via shield bashing. You have to push your opponent outside the circle. I didn't do too great, was too easily pushed over. Didn't brace hard enough.

    Entry 30 (11/22/1533) (1BU)¶

    Life class week. We were all given a small potted tree, and had to make it grow. Cobalt had little issue, she just forgot to give it enough water. I think I used too much water... John, given a plant too for some reason, poured some of the water in and said that he has "made the plant grow." Mr. Davis gave him a look, but agreed. I wonder why he opts to take the focus classes.

    Entry 31 (11/23/1533) (1BU)¶

    ...

    Entry 32 (11/24/1533) (1BU)¶

    Fell asleep IN my journal last night.

    Entry 33 (12/6/1533) (1BU)¶

    Winter break is coming soon, but combat class waits for no kni(or man). Our combat focus teacher called me over. She had me form my shield...

    ...

    And had Jessie (reluctantly) push me over.

    Yes, I get it, bracing is important.

    Entry 34 (12/13/1533) (1BU)¶

    Mom sat me down today, and told me about being claimed. Our hope is starting to slip. There are still searching parties looking for him, but they still haven't found anything.

    Mom says that when a kni gets too weak, or looses too much essence, their energy can be claimed by another kni. Usually, this happens naturally. An old kni will pass his or her energy on to his or her alloys. The claiming process leaves behind a white, ashy residue where the kni used to be. She said that, at this point, that's what the guard is looking for.

    I hope not.

    Entry 35 (12/20/1533) (1BU)¶

    Ferrite said Cobalt's birthday is on the 22nd. He's a cool guy, but sometimes a little cold. Quite protective of Cobalt. Not sure why, I didn't ask.

    (Ferrite Wilton)
    Cool guy, but quite guarded. Ferrite's a humanlike kni with black hair. He looks rather strong, and it's a little intimidating. He's Cobalt's older brother.

    Entry 36 (12/27/1533) (1BU)¶

    The winter solstice wasn't the same without dad.

    I made sure not to let Cobalt and Ferrite down. Mainly Ferrite. He looks like he could probably fold me in half if he needed to...

    Entry 37 (1/3/1534) (0BU)¶

    It's the new year! It's now 1534AM. That's "After Man," not time. Time-wise, it's about 11pm.

    There's traces of what looks like flour in the snow. That's odd.

    Entry 38 (1/10/1534) (0BU)¶

    I could've sworn I smelled something burning the other day.

    Ok, that's not snow...

    Entry 39 (1/17/1534) (0BU)¶

    There have been reports of blackfires in the nearby woods. Well, that's one mystery solved. Another started, what is a blackfire?? The guard is doubling. We are to expect a replica kni attack. Dad is a military officer. His being missing doesn't fill us with confidence...

    Entry 40 (1/24/1534) (0BU)¶

    There's been rumors of replica kni in the city perimeter. Mom says they're just rumors, and that she hasn't seen any.

    Entry 41 (1/31/1534) (0BU)¶

    Well, that's unmistakable. Zinc and I took a bit of a bicycle ride against out better judgement. The outskirts of the city are being burned with what looks like black fire.

    OOOHHHH

    There wasn't anyone around, maybe they already got an evacuation notice out to the people there.

    Entry 42 (2/7/1534) (0BU)¶

    The guard has released a warning confirming sightings of replica kni INSIDE the city...

    That's...

    That's not good.

    Entry 43 (2/14/1534) (0BU)¶

    Zinc and I bike over to what the guard's been calling "the perimeter" now and then. They've sent out a memo telling everyone that can to stay within their homes. But the guard is getting tired. Zinc and I watching from as close as we dare...There's a lot of wounded people. A healer told us to go home.

    If it keeps like this, would we be safe there?

    The ash is..."whiter" today. It almost looks like snow, but it doesn't pack.

    Entry 44 (2/21/1534) (0BU)¶

    (There's a piece of paper taped into the journal.)

    "CRYSTALLYNE GUARD PUBLIC MEMO

    As you've probably heard from the rumors, the guard is wearing out. We've entered talks with the attacking replica kni. Their demands for peace are...disturbing. And what's worse is that they don't seem to be willing to compromise yet.

    We will continue peace talks as time goes on. If you are able, please continue to stay in your houses. Your safety is our priority.

    ~ The Crystallyne Guard"

    This was in the mail today. Mom is worried. I can see why.

    Entry 45 (2/28/1534) (0BU)¶
